reticulum-rnode Flash firmware, provision identity, and configure your RNode over USB

Your browser does not support Web Serial. Use a Chromium-based browser (Chrome, Edge, Opera, Brave) on desktop.

What this does

Flash the reticulum-rnode firmware onto an nRF52840 + SX1262 board straight from this page, then connect over USB to provision the device identity, configure radio parameters, and save settings to flash. The device acts as a KISS serial LoRa modem for Reticulum's RNodeInterface.

Flash firmware

Pick a version below and click Select firmware. Then put the board in bootloader mode by double-tapping the reset button (within ~500 ms) — the device will re-enumerate as a new USB serial port. Click Flash and pick that new port in the browser's picker.

Or upload a local firmware.zip (for developers building their own)
